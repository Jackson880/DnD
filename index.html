Languages Abyssal, Infernal It can learn any language within 3 days of hearing it
Challenge 12 (8,400 XP)
Proficiency Bonus +4

Aversion of Fire. If the tomb guardian takes fire damage, it has advantage on attack rolls and ability checks until the end of its next turn.

Immutable Form. The tomb guardian is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

Necrotic Absorption. Whenever the tomb guardian is subjected to necrotic damage, it takes no damage and instead regains a number of hit points equal to the necrotic damage dealt.

Magic Resistance. The guardian has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Pentecostal Transmute (Can Only Be Used Once). This Demon will transmute back into its body 1 hour after it dies.

Pentecostal Hunt. This Demon will seek out the being that killed it and will not stop until that being dies or the Demonic Guardian dies again.

Multiattack. The tomb guardian makes two bite attacks.

Legendary Actions

The demonic guardian can take 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creatureâ€™s turn. The demonic guardian regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Demonic Transmove. The guardian "teleports" 30ft away. If a player is in-between the old location and the new location it needs to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or they are set ablaze.

Demonic Shape. The Demonic Guardian can change its shape to one living creature in a 30ft radius, Causing you to hit the creature it changed into if you don't pass a perception check. It doesn't have to be in its original form to change and can use this action whenever to shift back into its original form.

Description

The Demonic Guardian's originates from another plain of existence and is brought to our plain occasionally by "failed summonings of Satan". It appears to be extremally volatile. If they had any Intelligence it was lost on the trip here as now all it wants to do is devour a endless amount of flesh. The body of the Guardian is made out of pure muscle, dead heartstring, or string-like demons and is thus able to adapt and change its body within days of living in a environment.
